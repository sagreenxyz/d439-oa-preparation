# Question 20

A nurse received change-of-shift reports on these four patients and started rounding. Which patient does the nurse need to focus on as a priority?

**Type:** Single choice
**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- A patient who had abdominal surgery 2 days ago and is requesting pain medication
- CORRECT ==> A patient admitted yesterday with atrial fibrillation who now has a decreased level of consciousness
- A patient with a wound drain who needs teaching before discharge in the early afternoon
- A patient going to surgery for a mastectomy in 3 hours who has a question about the surgery
