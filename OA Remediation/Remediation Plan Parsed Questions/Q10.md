# Question 10

A patient comes to an urgent care clinic with reports of pain in the right lower calf and ankle after participating in a 5K run. Which of the following assessment questions will determine the effects exercise has had on this patient?

**Type:** Single choice
**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- Tell me specifically when your pain began.
- Describe for me the pain you are having.
- CORRECT ==> In what way has your daily activity changed since you noticed your pain?
- How long have you been having the pain?
