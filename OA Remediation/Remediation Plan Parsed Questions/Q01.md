# Question 1

During the administration of a warm tap-water enema, a patient starts to have cramping abdominal pain that he rates a 6 out of 10. What nursing action should the nurse take first?

**Type:** Single choice
**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- CORRECT ==> Stop the installation.
- Ask the patient to take deep breaths to decrease the pain.
- Tell the patient to bear down as he would when having a bowel movement.
- Continue the installation; then administer a pain medication.
