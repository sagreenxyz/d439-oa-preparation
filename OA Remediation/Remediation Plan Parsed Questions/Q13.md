# Question 13

A patient states, "I do not have confidence in my doctor. She looks so young." What is the nurse's therapeutic response?

**Type:** Single choice
**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- CORRECT ==> Tell me more about your concern.
- You have nothing to worry about. Your doctor is perfectly competent.
- You can go online and see how others have rated your doctor. I do that.
- You should ask your doctor to tell you her background.
