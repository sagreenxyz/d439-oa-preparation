# Question 16

The infection control nurse has asked the staff to work on reducing the number of iatrogenic infections on the unit. Which of the following actions on the nurses' part would contribute to reducing health care–acquired infections? Select all that apply.

**Type:** Multiple choice
**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- CORRECT ==> Teaching correct handwashing to assigned patients
- CORRECT ==> Using correct procedures in starting and caring for an intravenous infusion
- CORRECT ==> Providing perineal care to a patient with an indwelling urinary catheter
- CORRECT ==> Isolating a patient on antibiotics who has been having loose stool for 24 hours
- Decreasing a patient's environmental stimuli to decrease nausea
