# Question 9

The nurse is providing education on STIs to a group of older adults. The nurse knows that further teaching is needed when the participants make which statements? Select all that apply.

**Type:** Multiple choice
**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- CORRECT ==> "I don't need to use condoms since there is no risk for pregnancy."
- "I should be screened for an STI each time I'm with a new partner."
- CORRECT ==> "I know I'm not infected because I don't have discharge or sores."
- CORRECT ==> "I was tested for STIs last year, so I know I'm not infected."
- CORRECT ==> "The infection rate in older adults is low because most are not sexually active."
