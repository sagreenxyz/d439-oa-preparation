# Question 26

A patient who is depressed is crying and verbalizes feelings of low self-esteem and self-worth, such as "I am such a failure. I cannot do anything right." What is the nurse's best response?

**Type:** Single choice
**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- CORRECT ==> Remain with the patient until the patient validates feeling more stable.
- Tell the patient that this is not true and that every person has a purpose in life.
- Review recent behaviors or accomplishments that demonstrate skill ability.
- Reassure the patient that you know how he or she is feeling and that things will get better.
