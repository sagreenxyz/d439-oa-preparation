# Question 17

Which of these statements are true regarding disinfection and cleaning? Select all that apply.

**Type:** Multiple choice
**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- CORRECT ==> Proper cleaning requires mechanical removal of all soil from an object or area.
- CORRECT ==> Routine environmental cleaning is an example of medical asepsis.
- When cleaning a wound, wipe around the wound edge first and then clean inward toward the center of the wound.
- CORRECT ==> Cleaning in a direction from the least to the most contaminated area helps reduce infections.
- Disinfecting and sterilizing medical devices and equipment involve the same procedures.
