# Question 18

A nurse is taking a sleep history from a patient. The nurse recognizes that which statement made by the patient needs further follow-up?

**Type:** Single choice
**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- "I feel refreshed when I wake up in the morning."
- "I use soft music at night to help me relax."
- CORRECT ==> "It takes me about 45 to 60 minutes to fall asleep."
- "I take the pain medication for my leg pain about 30 minutes before I go to bed."
