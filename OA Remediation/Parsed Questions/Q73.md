# Question 73

**Prompt:** A nurse is caring for a client who was newly diagnosed with amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS). The client states, “I don’t see any reason to keep trying anymore.” Which response by the nurse demonstrates the most effective open-ended therapeutic communication ?
**Type:** Single choice.

## Options
1. “You have many treatment options that can help slow the disease.”
2. “Why do you feel like there is no reason to continue?"
3. “Describe what you are experiencing since receiving this diagnosis.”
4. “You shouldn’t give up before exploring all available resources.”
