# Question 51

**Prompt:** A post-op client is using a PCA pump for pain control. The nurse notices the family frequently pressing the PCA button for the client. What is the best action?
**Type:** Single choice.

## Options
1. Educate the family that only the client should press the PCA button
2. Disable the pump and notify the provider
3. Increase the PCA dose and monitor closely
4. Place the button out of reach of the family
