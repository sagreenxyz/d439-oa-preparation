# Question 75

**Prompt:** A client on a clear liquid diet asks if they can have a popsicle. What is the nurse’s best response?
**Type:** Single choice.

## Options
1. “Only fruit-flavored popsicles without pulp are allowed.”
2. “Popsicles are not allowed on a clear liquid diet.”
3. “You may have any popsicles as long as they are sugar-free.”
4. “Popsicles are allowed only after your next meal.”
