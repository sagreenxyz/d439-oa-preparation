# Question 61

**Prompt:** A client goal was “pain will be ≤ 3/10 after medication.” After administration, client reports pain is 7/10. What is the nurse’s next step?
**Type:** Single choice.

## Options
1. Document as goal met
2. Reassess pain in 2 hours
3. Notify the provider and reassess the plan
4. Re-educate the client on pain scale
