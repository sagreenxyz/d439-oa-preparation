# Question 14

To allow adequate ventilation and oxygenation during nasopharyngeal suctioning, which action should the nurse take?

**Type:** Single choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- Perform at least four suctioning passes but no more than five passes with the suction catheter.
- Allow adequate time between suctioning passes
- Suction again immediately after a pass to minimize the overall procedure time.
- Perform at least three suctioning passes but no more than four passes with the suction catheter.
