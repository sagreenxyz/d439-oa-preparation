# Question 48

An anxious client repeatedly uses the call bell to get the nurse to come to the room. Finally, the nurse says to the client, “If you keep ringing, there will come a time when I won’t answer your bell.” Which legal term is related to this statement?

**Type:** Single choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- Slander
- Battery
- CORRECT ==> Assault
- Libel
