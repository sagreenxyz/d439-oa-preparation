# Question 1

A nurse is providing care to a client with an indwelling urinary catheter. Which of the following actions should the nurse perform to prevent catheter-associated urinary tract infections (CAUTI)?  Select all that apply.

**Type:** Multiple choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- Keep the drainage bag below the level of the bladder at all times
- Clamp the catheter tubing every 4 hours to allow the bladder to fill
- Clean the catheter insertion site daily with soap and water
- Disconnect the catheter tubing frequently for cleaning
- Secure the catheter to the client’s thigh to prevent tension on the tubing
