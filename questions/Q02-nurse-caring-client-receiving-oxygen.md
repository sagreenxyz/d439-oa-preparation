# Question 2

A nurse is caring for a client receiving oxygen via nasal cannula at 4 L/min. During the morning assessment, the nurse notes redness and irritation around the client's nares and behind the ears. Which of the following is the priority nursing action?

**Type:** Single choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- Apply a water-soluble lubricant to the client’s nostrils and document the finding
- Secure the cannula tubing more tightly to prevent movement and dislodgement
- Notify the healthcare provider and request an order for humidified oxygen
- CORRECT ==> Pad the tubing with gauze or foam and reassess skin frequently for breakdown
