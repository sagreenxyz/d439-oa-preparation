# Question 37

A nurse finds that a terminally ill patient has cold, clammy, and wax-like skin. What should the nurse infer from this assessment

**Type:** Single choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- The patient is improving.
- CORRECT ==> The patient is likely to die soon.
- The patient has edema and needs diuretics.
- The intravenous fluids have extravasated.
