# Question 7

A nurse is caring for a patient who does not speak English. After teaching the patient about wound care, which action best evaluates the patient’s understanding?

**Type:** Single choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- Ask the patient, “Do you understand how to care for your wound?”
- CORRECT ==> Use a professional interpreter to have the patient explain the wound care steps back to the nurse
- Provide written wound care instructions in English only
- Observe the patient’s facial expressions during teaching for signs of understanding
