# Question 35

A mother whose young daughter has died of leukemia is crying and is unable to talk about her feelings. Which is the best response by the nurse?

**Type:** Single choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- “Everyone will remember her because she was so cute. She was one of our favorites."
- “As hard as this is, it is probably for the best because she was in a lot of pain.”
- “She put up the good fight, but now she is out of pain and in heaven.”
- “It must be hard to deal with such a precious loss.”
