# Question 15

A nurse is preparing to obtain a tympanic temperature from a 4-year-old child who is febrile. Which of the following actions should the nurse take to ensure the most accurate reading?

**Type:** Single choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- Pull the pinna down and back before inserting the thermometer
- Place the probe loosely at the opening of the ear canal
- Gently pull the pinna up and back before inserting the thermometer
- Insert the thermometer probe deeply into the ear canal to obtain a core temperature
