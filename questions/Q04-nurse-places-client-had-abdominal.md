# Question 4

The nurse places a client who had abdominal surgery in the semi-Fowler position. The client states, “I prefer to lie flat when in bed. I feel like I am almost sitting up.” Which is the most appropriate response by the nurse ?

**Type:** Single choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- “Raising the head of your bed supports your breathing.”
- “Keeping your upper body elevated encourages urinary elimination.”
- “Increasing the height of the top of the bed promotes your passing gas.”
- “Lifting your chest above your abdomen facilitates drainage in your wound drains.”
