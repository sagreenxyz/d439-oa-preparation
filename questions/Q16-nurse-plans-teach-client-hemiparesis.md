# Question 16

The nurse plans to teach a client with hemiparesis to use a cane. Which should the nurse teach the client to do?

**Type:** Single choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- Move forward 1 step with the weak leg first, followed by the strong leg and cane.
- Adjust the cane height 12 inches lower than the waist.
- Hold the cane in the strong hand when walking
- Lean over onto the cane when walking.
