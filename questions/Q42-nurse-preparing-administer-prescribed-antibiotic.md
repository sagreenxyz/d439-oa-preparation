# Question 42

A nurse is preparing to administer a prescribed antibiotic ear drop to an adult client with external otitis. Which of the following actions by the nurse demonstrates correct technique?

**Type:** Single choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- Pull the pinna down and back to straighten the ear canal
- Keep the client in a side-lying position with the affected ear up for 2–3 minutes after administration
- Hold the ear drop bottle in the refrigerator until administration to preserve potency
- Insert a cotton-tipped applicator to push the medication deeper into the ear canal
