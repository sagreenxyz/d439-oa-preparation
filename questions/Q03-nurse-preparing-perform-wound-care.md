# Question 3

A nurse is preparing to perform wound care using medical asepsis. Which of the following actions should the nurse take to maintain medical asepsis?

**Type:** Multiple choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- Wash hands for at least 20 seconds before the procedure
- Keep sterile supplies below waist level
- Pour sterile solution onto a sterile field
- Touch the inside of the sterile dressing wrapper while applying the dressing
