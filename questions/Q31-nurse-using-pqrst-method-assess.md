# Question 31

A nurse is using the PQRST method to assess an adult client’s pain. Which question corresponds to the “S” in PQRST?

**Type:** Single choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- What does the pain feel like? (sharp, dull, burning)
- What makes the pain better or worse?
- Where is the pain located, and does it radiate?
- On a scale of 0 to 10, how severe is your pain?
