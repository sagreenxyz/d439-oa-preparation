# Question 47

A nurse is calling the healthcare provider to report a change in a post-operative client's condition using the SBAR framework. Which of the following statements made by the nurse best reflects the "Recommendation" component?

**Type:** Single choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- "The client is a 72-year-old who had a total hip arthroplasty 24 hours ago." \[this is background\]
- "Vital signs are: BP 88/54, HR 112, and the surgical dressing is saturated with blood." \[this is assessment\]
- CORRECT ==> "I believe the client is experiencing postoperative hemorrhage and needs immediate intervention." \[this is recommendation\]
- "The client became hypotensive and tachycardic during my shift." \[this is situation\]

## Notes

> **This would be the SBAR report:**
> 
> "The client became hypotensive and tachycardic during my shift. The client is a 72-year-old who had a total hip arthroplasy 24 hours ago. Vital signs are: BP 88/54, HR 112, and the surgical dressing is saturated with blood. I believe the client is experiencing postoperative hemorrhage and needs immediate intervention."
