# Question 24

A nurse is providing range-of-motion exercises for a 53-year-old female patient who is recovering from a stroke. During the session, the patient complains that she is "too tired to go on." What would be priority nursing actions for this patient? Select all that apply

**Type:** Multiple choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- Stop performing the exercises.
- Decrease the number of repetitions performed
- Re-evaluate the nursing plan of care
- Move to the patient's other side to perform exercises.
- Encourage the patient to finish the exercises and then rest.
- Assess the patient for other symptoms.
