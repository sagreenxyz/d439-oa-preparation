# Question 26

During a sudden hospital power outage, the nurse is unable to access the Electronic Health Record (EHR) system to document a client’s change in condition. What is the nurse’s best action to ensure accurate and legal documentation?

**Type:** Single choice

**Points:** 2 Points

## Options

- Wait until the power is restored to document the assessment in the EHR
- Verbally report the client’s condition to the healthcare provider and delay documentation
- CORRECT ==> Document the assessment on paper using the facility’s downtime forms and enter it into the EHR later
- Rely on memory to complete the documentation once the EHR is available
